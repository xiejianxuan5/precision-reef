The Biological Bottleneck

For decades, reef keepers have obsessed over PAR (Photosynthetically Active Radiation). However, 2026 research indicates that static PAR levels are a primary cause of Zooxanthellae stress. In the wild, corals are exposed to shifting spectral qualities dictated by tidal movements, turbidity, and atmospheric refraction.

PUR vs. PAR: A Critical Distinction

PONTIS prioritizes PUR (Photosynthetically Usable Radiation). By focusing energy specifically on the chlorophyll a and c2 absorption peaks, the PONTIS AI engine reduces heat waste while maximizing fluorescence—the vibrant "glow" every reefer chases.
