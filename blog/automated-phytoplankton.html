Scalable Live Food Production: Automated Phytoplankton Cultivation Systems

Published Feb 15, 2026 • By PrecisionReef Labs

The Foundation of the Marine Food Web

Phytoplankton is the bedrock of any marine ecosystem. In traditional reef keeping, maintaining high-density cultures of Nannochloropsis or Tetraselmis has been a labor-intensive chore prone to "crash" events. PrecisionReef is revolutionizing this with the CYAN-Reactor, an automated photobioreactor designed for 24/7 continuous cultivation.

Precision Gas Exchange & CO2 Optimization

Traditional "bubblers" are inefficient. High-density cultivation requires precise CO2 injection to maintain pH levels within the 7.8 to 8.2 range, ensuring maximum carbon fixation. Our system uses optical density (OD) sensors to monitor cell density in real-time, adjusting nutrient dosing and gas exchange dynamically.

AI-Driven Harvest Cycles

Using the PrecisionReef Core API, users can now automate the "Continuous Drip" method, feeding the reef tank directly from the reactor without manual intervention. This eliminates the nutritional decay associated with refrigerated phytoplankton products.

Sustainable Scaling

Scalability isn't just about bigger tanks; it's about robust logic. By decentralizing live food production, we reduce the carbon footprint of refrigerated shipping and provide corals with the freshest possible nutrients, leading to unprecedented growth rates and polyp extension.
